Akan Names.

Ever heard of Akan names??

The tradition goes back to the Akan tribe: to name their children by the day of their birth. However, Ghanaian names have an even deeper meaning. We take you into this culture and show you as well your Ghanaian name! The Akan, culturally and linguistically related tribes in West Africa, do the same as other African and Jamaican ethnic groups: Name their children after the respective day they were born. Over time, the Akan have defined seven names per gender, each referring to a day of the week:

Male Akan-Names

  • Kwasi
  • Kwadwo
  • Kwabena
  • Kwaku
  • Yaw
  • Kofi
  • Kwame

Female Akan-Names

  • Akosua
  • Adwoa
  • Abenaa
  • Akua
  • Yaa
  • Afua
  • Ama

This naming after the days of the week has a much deeper meaning, because the day on which a person was born has about the similar significance as in the western hemisphere the assignment to a sign of the zodiac.
